Phosphorylation Protocol

T4 polynucleotide kinase
10,000 units/mL
Stored at -20C

Catalyzes the transfer and exchange of Pi from the gamma position of ATP to the 5'hydroxyl terminus of polynucleotides for further ligation

Protocol:

Total volume: 50 uL
T4 PNK 1 µl (10 units)
10X T4 PNK Buffer 5 µl
10 mM ATP 5 µl (1 mM final conc.)
DNA (20 mer) 10 uL(1-2 µg )
Nuclease-free Water Fill to 50 µl
Incubation 37°C for 30 minutes
Heat inactivate (optional) 65C for 20 min

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Ligation Protocol:

total volume: 20 uL
PCR product 2-5 uL
Reaction buffer (2x) 10 uL
T4 ligase 1 uL
Sterile water fill up to 20 uL

1. Mix gentally by pipetting up and down and microfuge briefly
2. incubate @ 16C O/N or room temperature for 2 h
3. (optional: Heat inactivate @ 65C for 10 min)
4. Chill on ice and transform 1-5 uL of the reaction into 50 uL competent cells (alternatively, you can store the ligation product at -20C and do the transformation on the second day)
